What is Gyyti?

Gyyti is a mobile application that transforms empty car seats into part of public transportation. It functions as a real-time marketplace that connects drivers and passengers, turning traditional carpooling into a smart, demand-driven form of public transit. By making travel faster, more convenient, and environmentally friendly, Gyyti offers a competitive alternative to traditional public transport providers. The platform addresses growing consumer environmental awareness and leverages technology to modernize how people move, following trends already established in Central Europe.

The platform

Gyyti is a real-time, user-driven platform that connects commuters, basically drivers and passengers safely and efficiently. Designed for trust, convenience, and speed, it redefines everyday travel through smart, secure carpooling.
